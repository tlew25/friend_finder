Welcome

Posey is our little Crackerdog. She is a 25lb Schipperke mix that we rescued from Town Lake Animal Shelter. Posey is "going crackerdog" when she gets a burst of energy and runs around really fast all over for no reason. It is sort of a happy dance. She has always done this but we never had a name for it until we heard the term in Dog Stories by James Harriot. Our logo is a picture of Posey going Crackerdog.
